## Introduction

Welcome to the Bakery ERP System User Guide! This comprehensive document will help you master all features of our professional management solution designed specifically for bakery businesses.

### What is the Bakery ERP System?

The Bakery ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) System is a comprehensive solution that helps you manage every aspect of your bakery business:

- **Product Management**: Organize and manage your bakery's product catalog
- **Order Processing**: Create and track customer orders efficiently
- **Inventory Control**: Monitor stock levels and prevent stockouts
- **Financial Tracking**: Record payments and manage billing
- **Analytics & Reporting**: Understand your business performance with detailed reports
- **Customer Management**: Keep track of customer information and preferences

### Who Should Use This Guide?

This guide is for:
- New users setting up their account
- Existing users looking to master advanced features
- Managers and team members responsible for different departments
- Anyone needing help with specific tasks or troubleshooting

3. **Enter Password**: Your secure password (case-sensitive)
4. **Click Login**: Submit your credentials
5. **Verify**: Complete any verification steps if needed

The system uses modern security measures to protect your data:

- **Secure Login**: HTTPS encryption for all connections
- **Password Protection**: Strong password requirements enforced
- **Two-Factor Authentication**: Optional 2FA for added security

- Minimum 8 characters

- At least one lowercase letter

4. Follow the setup instructions
5. Save backup codes in a secure location

## Product Management

### Creating a Product

#### Step-by-Step Process

1. **Go to Products** menu
2. Click **"Add Product"** button
3. **Fill in Product Details**:
   - Product Name (required)
   - Description
   - Category (select or create)
   - SKU (unique identifier)

4. **Set Pricing**:
   - Cost Price (what you pay to make it)
   - Selling Price (customer price)

5. **Upload Image** (optional)
   - Recommended size: 500x500px
   - Supported formats: JPG, PNG

6. **Inventory Settings**:
   - Initial Stock Quantity
   - Reorder Level (alert threshold)

7. **Click Save**

### Editing Products

1. Go to Products list
2. Find product to edit
3. Click **Edit** icon (pencil)
4. Make necessary changes
5. **Click Save**

## Order Management

### Creating an Order

#### Complete Workflow

1. **Navigate to Orders** menu
2. Click **"Create New Order"** button
3. **Select or Create Customer**:
   - Choose existing customer, OR
   - Click "Add New Customer"
   - Fill in customer details

4. **Add Products to Order**:
   - Click "Add Item"
   - Search for product
   - Enter quantity
   - Repeat for additional items

5. **Set Order Details**:
   - Order Date
   - Delivery Date
   - Special Notes
   - Apply Discount (if applicable)

6. **Select Payment Method**:
   - Cash
   - Credit Card
   - Check
   - Bank Transfer
   - Other

7. **Review and Create**:
   - Review order summary
   - Click "Create Order"

### Order Statuses

| Status | Meaning | Next Action |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| Draft | Order not confirmed | Complete order |
| Pending | Awaiting processing | Prepare items |
| Processing | Being prepared | Continue prep |
| Ready | Ready for pickup/delivery | Arrange pickup |
| Shipped | Out for delivery | Track delivery |
| Delivered | Successfully delivered | Confirm with customer |
| Cancelled | Order cancelled | Process refund if needed |

### Tracking Orders

**Finding an Order:**
1. Go to **Orders** menu
2. Use search bar to find by:
   - Order ID
   - Customer Name
   - Date Range

3. Click order to view details
4. See full timeline and history

### Recording Payments

1. Open the order

3. Enter payment details:
   - Amount paid

4. Click **"Save Payment"**

## Inventory Tracking

### Understanding Inventory

#### Key Inventory Terms

- **Current Stock**: Amount on hand right now
- **Reorder Level**: Minimum before alert
- **Reserved Stock**: Items allocated to orders
- **Available Stock**: Current - Reserved
- **Stock Turnover**: How quickly items sell

### Updating Stock

#### Manual Update
1. Go to **Inventory** menu
2. Find product
3. Click **"Update Stock"**
4. Enter new quantity or adjustment amount
5. Add note (reason for change)
6. Click **"Save"**

### Low Stock Alerts

#### Setting Reorder Levels
1. Go to **Products**
2. Open product to edit
3. Find "Reorder Level" field
4. Enter minimum quantity
5. Click **Save**
6. You'll receive alerts when stock drops below this

#### Alert Types
- **Low Stock**: Below reorder level - place order soon
- **Out of Stock**: Zero quantity - emergency order needed
- **Overstock**: Well above normal - consider promotions

### Stock Adjustments

#### Common Reasons to Adjust Stock

| Reason | Action | Example |
|--------|--------|---------|
| Damaged Items | Reduce stock | Remove 5 damaged croissants |
| Theft/Loss | Reduce stock | Missing inventory -3 |
| New Delivery | Add stock | Received shipment +50 |
| Inventory Error | Correct | Recount showed difference |
| Transfer | Adjust | Move between locations |

#### Making Adjustments
1. Go to **Inventory → Adjustments**
2. Click **"New Adjustment"**
3. Select product and reason
4. Enter quantity
5. Add detailed note
6. Click **Save**

### Physical Stock Count

#### Conducting a Count
1. Go to **Inventory → Stock Count**
2. Click **"Start New Count"**
3. Select products to count
4. Print count sheet
5. Count items physically
6. Enter actual quantities
7. Review discrepancies
8. Approve adjustments

#### Recommended Frequency
- **Cyclic Count**: Weekly or bi-weekly (portion of inventory)
- **Full Count**: Monthly or quarterly (all items)

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues & Solutions

#### Login Problems

**Issue: "Invalid Email or Password"**
- Check email spelling
- Verify CAPS LOCK is off
- Confirm password is correct
- Try "Forgot Password" if unsure

**Issue: "Account Locked"**
- Account locked after 5 failed attempts
- Wait 30 minutes before trying again
- Use "Forgot Password" to reset
- Contact support if needed

**Issue: Not Receiving Reset Email**
- Check spam/junk folder
- Add no-reply@bakerysystem.com to contacts
- Wait 5-10 minutes and check again
- Request another reset link

#### System Performance

**Issue: Page Loading Slowly**
- Clear browser cache
- Try different browser
- Check internet connection
- Restart your device

**Issue: Features Not Loading**
- Refresh the page (Ctrl+R)
- Clear cookies and cache
- Try in incognito/private mode
- Disable browser extensions

#### Data Issues

**Issue: Missing or Incorrect Data**
- Verify data was saved
- Check if page refreshed
- Log out and log back in
- Contact support with details

**Issue: Inventory Not Updating**
- Refresh the page
- Check if you have permission
- Verify order didn't reserve items
- Contact support if persists

#### Payment Problems

**Issue: Payment Not Recorded**
- Verify amount entered correctly
- Check payment method selected
- Ensure order is in correct status
- Try again or contact support

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: What is included in my subscription?**
A: Your subscription includes unlimited products, orders, and customers, plus all features including reports and analytics.

**Q: Can I use the system on mobile?**
A: Yes! The system is fully responsive and works on all devices including phones and tablets.

**Q: How is my data backed up?**
A: Your data is automatically backed up multiple times daily to secure servers.

**Q: Can I export all my data?**
A: Yes, you can export data in various formats through the export functionality.

### Product Management

**Q: Can I delete a product?**
A: Yes, but we recommend disabling instead of deleting to maintain history. Once deleted, it cannot be recovered.

**Q: How do I handle seasonal products?**
A: Mark products as seasonal or create variants. You can also disable temporarily instead of deleting.

**Q: Can I import products from a file?**
A: Yes, use the bulk import feature with a CSV file.

### Order Management

**Q: Can I edit an order after creation?**
A: Yes, edit pending or processing orders. Completed orders can be viewed but not edited.

**Q: How do I handle cancellations?**
A: Click the Cancel button on the order. Optionally process refund and add cancellation notes.

**Q: Can customers track orders?**
A: Yes, customers receive email updates and can log in to track their orders.

### Inventory Management

**Q: Why is my system inventory different from physical?**
A: Common reasons: unrecorded sales, damaged items, data entry errors. Investigate and adjust to match physical count.

**Q: How often should I count inventory?**
A: Recommend weekly cyclic counts or monthly full counts depending on your business size.

**Q: Can I undo an adjustment?**
A: Yes, create a reverse adjustment with explanatory note.

### Reports & Analytics

**Q: How far back can I view data?**
A: You can view all data from when your account was created. Historical data is retained indefinitely.

**Q: Can I customize reports?**
A: Yes, you can select specific fields, date ranges, and filters for custom reports.

**Q: Can multiple people receive scheduled reports?**
A: Yes, add multiple email addresses to any scheduled report.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What browsers does the system support?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions recommended).

**Q: Is the system secure?**
A: Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures. All data transmitted over HTTPS.

**Q: What should I do if something isn't working?**
A: Try refreshing page, clearing cache, or using a different browser. If issue persists, contact support.
