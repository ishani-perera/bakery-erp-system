Tips & Tricks

Productivity Tips
  • Use keyboard shortcuts to navigate faster: Alt+P for Products, Alt+O for Orders
  • Save time by using bulk import to add multiple products at once
  • Set up inventory alerts to never run out of stock
  • Schedule automated reports to your email weekly
  • Use filters and tags to organize your products efficiently
