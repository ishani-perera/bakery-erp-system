Important

The Bakery ERP System uses secure authentication to protect your business data. Always use a strong password and keep your login credentials confidential.

Getting Started with Login

The login process is simple and straightforward. Here's what you need to know:

1

Access the Login Page

Navigate to the Bakery ERP System login page at: https://bakerysystem.com/login

  • You can bookmark this page for quick access
  • The page is mobile-friendly and works on all devices
2

Enter Your Credentials

Provide your login information:

  • Email Address: The email you used during registration
  • Password: Your secure password (case-sensitive)
3

Click Login

Click the "Login" button to access your account. You may be asked to verify your identity through:

  • Email verification (if it's a new device)
  • Two-factor authentication (if enabled)
4

Access Your Dashboard

Upon successful login, you'll be taken to your personalized dashboard where you can:

  • View your business summary and key metrics
  • Access recent orders and inventory
  • Navigate to different modules

Your First Login

Initial Setup Wizard

On your first login, you may see a setup wizard. Here's what to expect:

Step Description
Profile Setup Complete your business profile and contact information
Security Questions Set up security questions for account recovery
Notification Preferences Choose how you want to receive system notifications
API Keys Generate API keys if you plan to integrate with other systems
Pro Tip

Take time to complete the setup wizard thoroughly. This ensures your account is properly configured and secure.

Resetting Your Password

If you forget your password, don't worry! Follow these steps:

1

Click "Forgot Password?"

On the login page, click the "Forgot Password?" link below the login button.

2

Enter Your Email

Enter the email address associated with your account.

3

Check Your Email

Check your inbox for a password reset email from no-reply@bakerysystem.com. If you don't see it within 5 minutes, check your spam folder.

4

Click the Reset Link

Click the reset link in the email. The link is valid for 24 hours.

5

Create a New Password

Create a strong new password (minimum 8 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, and numbers).

Security Note

Password reset links are single-use only. If your link has expired, you'll need to start the process again.

Security Best Practices

DO's
  • ✓ Use a strong, unique password
  • ✓ Change password every 90 days
  • ✓ Enable two-factor authentication
  • ✓ Log out when done, especially on shared devices
  • ✓ Use HTTPS and secure networks
  • ✓ Update your browser regularly
DON'Ts
  • ✗ Share your password with anyone
  • ✗ Use the same password on multiple sites
  • ✗ Save passwords in unsecured text files
  • ✗ Log in from public or untrusted networks
  • ✗ Click suspicious links in emails
  • ✗ Leave your browser open and unattended

Password Requirements

Your password must meet these requirements:

  • Minimum 8 characters long
  • At least 1 uppercase letter (A-Z)
  • At least 1 lowercase letter (a-z)
  • At least 1 number (0-9)
  • At least 1 special character (!@#$%^&*)
  • Not your username or email address

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Two-Factor Authentication adds an extra layer of security to your account. Here's how to set it up:

Enable 2FA:

1
Go to Account Settings

Click your profile icon and select "Security Settings"

2
Select 2FA Method

Choose between:

  • Authenticator App: Use Google Authenticator, Microsoft Authenticator, or Authy
  • SMS Text Message: Receive codes via text
  • Email: Receive codes via email
3
Complete Verification

Scan the QR code with your authenticator app or verify your phone number

4
Save Recovery Codes

Save your backup recovery codes in a safe place. You'll need them if you lose access to your device.

Important

After enabling 2FA, you'll need to enter a verification code every time you log in. Keep your authentication device secure and accessible.

Troubleshooting Login Issues

  • Check that CAPS LOCK is not enabled

  • If you still can't log in, use "Forgot Password?" to reset it

  • Use "Forgot Password?" to reset your password
  • Contact support if you need immediate access
