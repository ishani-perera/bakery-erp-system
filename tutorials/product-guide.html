Product Management Guide

Master product creation, editing, and organization

Quick Navigation
Add Product Edit Product Categories Bulk Operations
Important

Products are the core of your bakery business. Ensure all products are properly categorized and priced for accurate reporting and inventory management.

Adding a New Product

1

Navigate to Products

Click on the Products menu item in the main navigation.

2

Click "Add New Product"

Click the green "Add Product" button in the top right corner.

3

Fill in Basic Information

Enter the product details:

  • Product Name: The name customers see (e.g., "Chocolate Croissant")
  • Description: Detailed description of the product
  • Category: Select or create a category
  • SKU: Unique product code (e.g., CC-001)
4

Set Pricing and Inventory

  • Cost Price: What you pay to make it
  • Selling Price: Price customers pay
  • Stock Quantity: Current quantity in stock
  • Reorder Level: Alert when stock is low
5

Upload Product Image

Click "Upload Image" to add a product photo. Recommended size: 500x500px.

6

Save Product

Click the "Save" button to add the product to your catalog.

Pro Tip

Use consistent SKU formats to make inventory management easier. Example: [CATEGORY]-[ITEM]-[VARIATION]

Editing Products

1

Go to Products List

Navigate to Products and find the product you want to edit.

2

Click Edit

Click the edit icon (pencil) next to the product name.

3

Make Changes

Update any product information:

  • Change pricing
  • Update description
  • Modify availability
  • Add or change image
4

Save Changes

Click "Save" to update the product. Changes take effect immediately.

Note

You can see edit history in the "Activity Log" tab to track all changes made to a product.

Bulk Operations

Manage multiple products at once to save time.

Bulk Import:

Import Products from CSV
  1. Go to Products → Import
  2. Download the template CSV file
  3. Fill in product information in the template
  4. Upload the file
  5. Review the preview and click "Import"

Required Columns: Product Name, Category, Cost Price, Selling Price, Stock Quantity

Bulk Update:

Update Multiple Products
  1. Select products from the list (checkbox)
  2. Click "Bulk Actions"
  3. Choose what to update: Price, Stock, Category, etc.
  4. Enter new values and click "Apply"

Bulk Delete:

Delete Multiple Products
  1. Select products to delete
  2. Click "Bulk Actions" → "Delete"
  3. Confirm deletion

Warning: Deleted products cannot be recovered. This action is permanent.

Best Practices

DO's
  • ✓ Use clear, descriptive product names
  • ✓ Keep SKUs consistent and logical
  • ✓ Update prices regularly
  • ✓ Use high-quality product images
  • ✓ Organize with categories
  • ✓ Set realistic reorder levels
DON'Ts
  • ✗ Use vague product names
  • ✗ Forget to update pricing
  • ✗ Leave products uncategorized
  • ✗ Use duplicate SKUs
  • ✗ Add poor quality images
  • ✗ Ignore low stock warnings
