Generate insights and make data-driven business decisions

Important

Reports provide valuable insights into your business performance. Use them to track trends, identify opportunities, and make informed decisions.

Reports Overview

The Reports section provides comprehensive business analytics through various reports:

Main Report Categories:

Sales Reports

Track revenue, sales trends, and customer behavior

Inventory Reports

Monitor stock levels and product movement

Performance Reports

Analyze business metrics and KPIs

Customer Reports

Understand customer patterns and preferences

1

2

Select the report category

3

Choose specific report

4

Set date range and filters

5

Click "Generate Report"

Sales Reports

Daily Sales Report:

What it shows: Total sales for each day

Includes: Revenue, number of orders, average order value

Best for: Daily business monitoring and tracking trends

Example: Monday: $500 revenue, 10 orders, $50 average

Monthly Sales Report:

What it shows: Sales summary for entire month

Includes: Total revenue, top products, busiest days

Best for: Performance tracking and planning

Example: January: $15,000 total, croissants top seller

Product Sales Report:

What it shows: Sales performance by product

Includes: Quantity sold, revenue per product, profit margins

Best for: Understanding which products are most profitable

Example: Chocolate Croissant: 500 units, $2,500 revenue, 40% margin

Customer Sales Report:

What it shows: Sales by customer

Includes: Total spent, number of orders, customer lifetime value

Best for: Identifying VIP customers and targeting efforts

Example: ABC Bakery: $5,000 spent, 50 orders over 6 months

Pro Tip

Use sales reports to identify your best-selling products and customers. Consider creating promotions for slow movers.

Inventory Reports

Stock Level Report:

What it shows: Current inventory levels

Includes: Quantity on hand, reorder level, stock value

Best for: Inventory overview and planning

Low Stock Alert Report:

What it shows: Products below reorder level

Includes: Current quantity, reorder level, recommended action

Best for: Planning reorders and avoiding stockouts

Stock Movement Report:

What it shows: How quickly items sell

Includes: Stock turn rate, movement trends

Best for: Identifying fast and slow movers

Inventory Value Report:

What it shows: Total value of inventory

Includes: Cost value, selling value, profit potential

Best for: Financial planning and asset tracking

Analytics Dashboard

The Analytics Dashboard provides real-time insights into your business performance.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):

KPI What It Means How to Improve
Total Revenue All sales income Increase prices or volume
Profit Margin Revenue minus costs Reduce costs or raise prices
Order Count Number of transactions Marketing and customer retention
Average Order Value Revenue per order Upsell and bundle offers
Customer Retention % of returning customers Loyalty programs, quality
Inventory Turnover How fast items sell Better forecasting, promotions

Scheduled Reports

Set up reports to be automatically generated and emailed to you on a schedule.

Setting Up Scheduled Reports:

1
Open Report

Generate the report you want to schedule

2
Click "Schedule"

Click the schedule button next to export

3
Set Frequency

Choose: Daily, Weekly, or Monthly

4
Add Recipients

Enter email addresses to receive reports

5
Save Schedule

Reports will be sent automatically

Note

You can manage all scheduled reports in the "Scheduled Reports" section.
